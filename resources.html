The situation surrounding education:

Education is one of the most important things we have, but there are thousands of people who lack literacy, and other forms of basic education.
There are many reasons for the education situation: including corrupted governments, poor families, bad schools, etc. In many countries, the
goverment funds well maintained public schools, but in many third world and some first world countries, due to the government corruption, public
schools are not in good shape. For richer families, this does not affect them much due to them joining private schools witha high tuition cost.
On the other hand poorer families, who lack proper funding, will have to stick with the government funded school. Adding on to this, many students
need to be transported for long periods of time to go to school, not having a school in their town! Families with a long line of people
with the same career path, such as fishing, will discourage their children from school, to learn the family practice. The cycle might not end
as the kids grow up and do the same thing to their children. A good education can lead to so many benefits for the individual and the
community as a whole. Decreasing the wealth gap, more stable and safe economies, booming innovation, and maybe the cure for cancer!
